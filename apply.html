Our Mission

To empower the excellent people of Gainesville, Florida to connect, create, and grow.

Nightjar aims to support and improve the accessibilty of artistic expression.

Our goal is to transform the space at 1628 NW 6th st to a dynamic center for visual arts, music, and education, and to provide inclusive opportunities for the community to engage, learn, and promote their own work.
