Gainesville Center for Artistic Practic

A project to acquire and operate permanent space to support a community-oriented art collective.

Our Mission

Vision: The Gainesville Center for Artistic Practice invisions a harmonious community where creativity flourishes, diversity is celebrated, and artistic expressions are accessible to all.


Goal: To transform the church space into a dynamic center for music, visual arts, and education, providing inclusive opportunities for the community to engage, learn, and grow. To inject accessibility and long-term stability to the arts and music lifeblood of Gainesville, FL.
