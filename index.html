Welcome to Nightjar

A center for artistic practice in Gainesville, FL.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative and sustainable solutions that empower businesses and communities to thrive.

Vision: The Gainesville Center for Artistic Practice invisions a harmonious community where creativity flourishes, diversity is celebrated, and artistic expressions are accessible to all.

Goal: To acquire and transform the old church on NW 6th St and 16th Ave into a dynamic center for music, visual arts, and education, providing inclusive opportunities for the community to engage, learn, and grow. To inject accessibility and long-term stability to the arts and music lifeblood of Gainesville, FL.
