Nightjar

A center for artistic practice in Gainesville, FL.

Our Mission

To empower people to connect, create, and grow.

Vision: Nightjar aims to support and improve the accessibilty of artistic expression in Gainesville, Florida.

Goal: To transform the space at 1628 NW 6th st to a dynamic center for music, visual arts, and education, and to provide inclusive opportunities for the community to engage, learn, and grow.

Principles

Growth, accessibility, and collaborative stewardship.

A Multi-purpose Space

Guided by Collaboration

There are three main usages of the space, with opportunities to cross-pollinate; Music practice and education, visual art studios, and the native plant nursery. A communal, web-based calendar and adherence to community guidelines allow artists to effectively share the space and thrive. Stewardship of the space is a collaborative effort of the users benefiting from the resources provided by Nightjar.

Our Offerings

Get involved and create art at Nightjar.

Shared Art Studio Space

Our daytime studio program provides shared studio space and resources for a variety of dry media, fabric arts, digital illustration, and artistic collaboration.

Music Practice Space

Teach private lessons or band practice. Use our community calendar to reserve space.

Bookings

Record music, hold meetings, produce video, or other activities that require full access to the building.
